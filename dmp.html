Double Money Program (DMP)

The Double Money Program (DMP) is an exciting upcoming feature on TIMBORD TEAM where users can participate in a transparent and rewarding funding circle.

Here's how it works: you invest a fixed amount of money and after a specific period, you receive double the amount you invested. For example, if you join the DMP with ₦5,000, you will receive ₦10,000 after your cycle completes.

The program is designed to reward early participants and will be based on a simple, verifiable queue system. Your referral strength can also help you earn faster rewards.

This program will have a detailed dashboard, status tracking, and user protection guidelines to ensure fairness for all users.
